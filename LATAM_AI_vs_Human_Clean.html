What the data says
AI is winning on output volume — and accelerating
890 FTDs vs 572 and 310 reactivations vs 67 over the same period. Conversion trend is sharply upward — Jan-26 hit 210 FTDs, Apr-26 hit 253 (new record). ai.daniela averages 42 reactivations/month and is still growing.
Humans generate 3–8× more CBV per outcome
achirino: $336 CBV per FTD vs AI's $106 (3.2×). grodriguez: $1,383 CBV per reactivation vs AI's $116 (8.2×). This is almost certainly client-quality not call-quality — humans work assigned high-value books.
The fix: tier the AI contact lists by client CBV
AI dials mass dormant lists. If higher-CBV dormant clients were prioritised in the queue, CBV/FTD would move toward $200–$300 without changing agent or script. This is a list quality problem, not an agent quality problem.
March dip was a campaign gap, not a trend
Mar-26 showed 87 AI FTDs. April rebounded to 253 — a programme record. Reading any single month in isolation is misleading. The 8-month trend is the signal.
